Hallmark
2016

When I arrived to film this commercial, the address we were given had two identical locations. I went to the wrong one. The cab dropped me off at the address - which was in a very nice neighborhood in Surrey, BC. Huge houses, manicured lawns. Being me, I was an hour early so I didn't immediately realize that I was in the wrong place. I sat on the curb, checked my phone and wandered a bit, waiting for someone to show up. A police cruiser approached me, asking why I was in the neighborhood. I explained my situation, and the cops told me one of the neighbors had called it in. A suspicious looking white guy who fit my description had been seen walking around very early in the morning. After answering about 20 questions, they let me go without incident. Director: Tony Barry
